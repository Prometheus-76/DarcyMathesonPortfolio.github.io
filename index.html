About Me

Background

I'm a 19 year old programmer based in Melbourne, Australia with a passion for games and over 4 years experience with Unity and C#. I am currently completing my second and final year of study at the Academy of Interactive Entertainment. I started making games around 7 years ago with GameMaker Studio 8 and haven't looked back since! My passion truly lies in programming and I love the wonderful problem-solving opportunities it presents and the creativity required to solve them!

My spare time is usually spent playing indie games, participating in game jams and working on side projects which can also be found here as I continue to work on them. My passion truly lies in game development and I love being able to design and develop fun experiences for others! I truly believe any problem can be overcome if you put your mind to it.

Qualifications & Experience

While in my final year of high school I also completed a Certificate III in Information, Digital Media and Technology, achieving a raw study score of 42, which placed me in the top 6% of the state for that subject! While at AIE I have also completed both the Diploma of Screen and Media, as well as the Diploma of Information Technology. By the end of 2022, I will have completed the Advanced Diploma of Professional Game Development as well.
