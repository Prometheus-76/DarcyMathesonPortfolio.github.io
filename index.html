Welcome to my portfolio!
I'm a 19 year old programmer from Melbourne, Australia with a passion for games
and over 4 years experience with Unity and C#. I started making games around 7 years ago
with GameMaker Studio 8 and haven't looked back since. I love programming and the
wonderful problem-solving oppotunities it can present in all kinds of situations!

My spare time is usually spent playing indie games and working on side projects
which can also be found here as I continue work on them.
